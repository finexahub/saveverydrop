The Mission

Born as a flagship CBSE Class X scientific initiative, Save Every Drop addresses the critical global challenge of water scarcity through innovative greywater filtration systems.

Our mission is to democratize water conservation technology, turning every household into a sustainable ecosystem of reuse and environmental stewardship.
